How It Works

Recycling your eWaste has never been easier. Follow our three-step process to make a positive impact on the environment.

Step 1: Submit a Request

Start your eWaste recycling journey by completing our online request form. Share details about the type and quantity of eWaste you have, along with your preferred pickup date and location.

This step allows us to plan efficiently and offer you a smooth experience.

Submit Request

Step 2: Pickup Scheduled

Once we receive your request, our team will confirm the details and schedule a convenient pickup time. You'll receive an email or text notification with all the necessary details.

Our professional team ensures safe handling of your eWaste while respecting your time and convenience.

Track Pickup

Step 3: Recycle Responsibly

Your collected eWaste is transported to our certified recycling facilities, where it undergoes environmentally safe processing. We prioritize extracting reusable materials while disposing of hazardous components responsibly.

By recycling with us, you're helping to reduce landfill waste and conserve valuable natural resources.
